## About

I work on language models with an architecture-first approach — implementing and instrumenting models from first principles to study how representations evolve under structural and capacity constraints.

Current focus: treating **pruning, quantization, and distillation as analytical probes** rather than purely engineering optimizations, to understand what compression reveals about how information is organized inside transformers.

```
Research interests: LLM architectures · Mechanistic interpretability · Model compression
                    Scaling laws · RAG systems · Edge AI · Indic NLP
```

## Research & Projects

### Architectural Information Bottlenecks in Compressed LMs
> *Active research — Department-led*

Studying how architectural choices and compression affect internal representations in transformer language models. Training compact transformers from scratch for controlled experimentation; applying pruning, quantization, and distillation as analytical interventions; instrumenting attention patterns and residual stream behavior across English and Indic language settings.

`PyTorch` `CUDA` `Transformers` `Mechanistic Interpretability`

---

### Agentic RAG for Safety-Critical Engineering Docs
> *Industry research — Moog India Technology Centre*

Agentic retrieval systems for multi-step reasoning over large structured aerospace engineering corpora. Query-aware routing, citation-grounded retrieval, and structured reasoning pipelines. Improved document retrieval accuracy from **~70% to 90%+**.

`LangChain` `LangGraph` `Vector Databases` `RAG`

---

### LLM Compression & Edge Deployment
> *Active — 2024–Present*

Compression and deployment experiments across 0.5B–7B parameter models. Built a teacher–student distillation pipeline (72B → student) for Hindi and Kannada low-resource instruction datasets. Deployed a 1B model on NVIDIA Jetson Nano (4GB RAM) for real-time on-device inference.

`QLoRA` `LoRA` `GGUF` `NVIDIA Jetson` `Indic NLP`

---

### Autonomous Drone Perception
> *Active — Sep 2025–Present*

Vision-based navigation pipelines for all-terrain UAVs — real-time obstacle detection, monocular depth estimation, and sensor fusion for autonomous flight in unstructured environments.

`OpenCV` `ROS2` `Depth Estimation` `Sensor Fusion`

---

### Humanoid Robotic Prosthetic Arm
> *Active — Jun 2025–Present*

Perception-driven servo control and actuation systems for humanoid prosthetic arm prototypes, integrating real-time visual feedback for adaptive grasping.

`ROS2` `Servo Control` `Computer Vision` `Hardware-in-the-Loop`
